CarHUD

CarHUD is a heads-up display for your vehicle. The app shows current notifications, media player information, and has a map with route guidance.

Check out the source code on bitbucket: https://github.com/madwindevelopers/carhud

Download: Google Play

Update 0.2.1

The update posted to Google Play today include a few visual enhancements and fixes.

  1. New launcher icon
  2. Removed shadow from navigation drawer (material design)
